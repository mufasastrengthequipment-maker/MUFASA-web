How to Buy — Step by Step
Go to SHOP NOW from the nav or home page.

Bundles group popular products at a discounted price. Filter by "Bundles" in the store to see all current offers. Bundle items are listed on the card — tap to order the whole set via WhatsApp in one message.

Alongside dumbbells, the store carries gym accessories (mats, benches, bands, etc.). Filter by "Accessories" to browse. Add to your cart alongside dumbbells for a combined order.

Monthly Competition
CHALLENGES

Join the monthly MUFASA challenge, track your daily progress, compete on the leaderboard, and win real prizes.

🏆
What Is the Challenge?

Each month a new challenge runs — typically 30 days. Members follow a linked workout plan and diet, log daily sessions, and the most consistent and improved participants win a cash prize paid via M-Pesa.

The current challenge name, dates, prize, and countdown are shown on the Challenges page.

📋
How to Join
Navigate to 🏆 Challenges in the nav bar.
Read the challenge description and check the end date and prize.
Tap "Join via WhatsApp" — this sends a pre-filled join message to the team.
You will be confirmed and added to the participants list.
📊
Progress Tracker
Tap "Start Tracking" — this redirects to the registration page. Complete your profile once.
Return daily and tap "Sign in with your phone number" to log in.
Fill in the daily log: Challenge day, body weight, DB weight used, session completion, how it felt, and energy level.
Tap "Submit Session" — your data is saved and visible to the admin for leaderboard rankings.
View your Progress Analysis below the log form — streaks, weight trend, session history.
📊
Leaderboard

The leaderboard below the tracker shows the top performers ranked by the admin based on consistency and progress. Rankings are updated regularly throughout the challenge month. Aim to appear on the podium — top 3 win prizes!

Win Tip: Logging every day — even rest days — shows consistency. Admins see your streak, so don't miss a day even if you only log a rest entry.
